# fresherlink
Student Features
1. Sign up / Login (JWT Auth) – Students can create accounts and log in securely
2. Profile Page – Displays student name, photo, and education info fetched from the database. 
3. Create profile– Students can create their profile with the institution name, department etc.
4. Profile Management – Students can update their personal or academic details and upload their resume to the server.
5. Apply for Job/Internship – Apply directly, application data is saved in the database. 
6. View Applied Jobs – Shows all the jobs the student has applied for. 
7. Manage Favourite Jobs – Students can save or remove job posts from their favourites list for easy access later.
8. Recommended Jobs – Shows similar jobs based on skills, role, or location. 
9. Job Details Page – Show complete job description and requirements.
10. Comment Management – Students can add or delete their comments on specific job circulars.
11. Apply Deadline Notification – Show students a countdown or alert for upcoming job/internship deadlines. 
12. Job application status- Students can see the status of their applications (Pending, Shortlisted, rejected)

Company Features 

1.Company Profile Management – Add or update logo, description, and details.

2. Job Post Management – Companies can create, update, or delete job and internship postings in the database.

3.Shortlist/Reject Applicants – Update applicant status (e.g., shortlisted/rejected)

4.Applicant Insights – Companies can see the total number of students who applied.


Admin Features 
1. Admin Login – Admin can log in using a separate role for control access. 
2. Manage Users – View, update, or delete student and company accounts. 
3. Manage Job Posts – Approve or reject jobs before showing publicly. 
4. Dashboard Statistics – Show total users, jobs, and companies with charts. 
5. Handle Reports – Manage reported users or posts for inappropriate  content. 
6. Send Announcements – Admin can broadcast important messages to all users.
